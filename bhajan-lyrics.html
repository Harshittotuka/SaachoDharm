Namokar Mantra

The most sacred mantra in Jainism, paying homage to the five supreme beings.

Lyrics

नमो अरिहंताणं नमो सिद्धाणं नमो आयरियाणं नमो उवज्झायाणं नमो लोए सव्व साहूणं एसो पंच नमोक्कारो सव्व पावप्पणासनो मंगलाणं च सव्वेसिं पढमं हवई मंगलं
Homage to the Arihants Homage to the Siddhas Homage to the Acharyas Homage to the Upadhyayas Homage to the Ascetics of the World This five-fold salutation Destroys all sin And of all auspicious things Is the foremost auspiciousness
